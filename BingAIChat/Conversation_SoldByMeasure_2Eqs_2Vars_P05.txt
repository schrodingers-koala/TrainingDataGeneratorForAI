Strawberry is sold by 67 cm3. The weight of strawberry is 70/67 g per cm3.
Blueberry is sold by 32 g. The volume of blueberry is 21/8 cm3 per g.
A shopper buys fruits at this market.
The sum of weight is 516 g.
The sum of volume is 654 cm3.
How much strawberry and blueberry does the shopper buy?
Show one solution.
