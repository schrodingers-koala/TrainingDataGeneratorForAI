The volume of one kiwifruit is 21 cm3. The volume of one apple is 34 cm3. Blueberry is sold by 16 g. The volume of blueberry is 1/4 cm3 per g. The volume of one melon is 49 cm3.
A shopper buys fruits at this market.
The sum of price is 155 cents.
The sum of weight is 270 g.
The sum of volume is 105 cm3.
How much kiwifruit, apple, blueberry and melon does the shopper buy?
Show one solution.
